Financing Policies:

  
We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Master card, Union Pay, Paypal. We also accept personal check for orders over $50.
 

Delivery Policies:

  
We offer free delivery for custumer within ten miles, and we charge $3 for every extra 5 miles. For grooming service, we offer free pick-up and delivery for customers who live within 20 miles.
 

Return Policies:

  
We accept any return item purchased within 5 days with tag on and original receipts. Full refund will be granted or you can save it as store credit.
